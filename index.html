Explore our interactive graph to see how breast cancer scientists work. Learn about their knowledge, the data they use, and the Bridge2AI scientists leading the way.

We use HetGNN (Heterogeneous Graph Neural Network) to represent node embeddings in the Breast Cancer area with the Pubmed Knowledge Graph Dataset. This includes five kinds of nodes: scientists, OBI methods, datasets, papers, and bioentities. Their relationships include writing, collaboration, mention, citing, and use.

We visualized scientists, methods, and datasets using K-means clustering (k=5) and T-SNE. The cluster labels are based on abstracts, titles, TF-IDF, and chatGPT.

At the bottom of this page, we list the main words of each cluster, including bio entities clusters.

How to interact with the graph?

  1. Use the mouse wheel or the ‘+’ and ‘-’ buttons on the top right of the canvas to zoom in and out.
  2. Hover over a node to see details.
  3. Double-click to autoscale and recover default settings.
  4. Click the legends to hide/show groups of nodes.
  5. Other tools are located on the top right of the canvas.
